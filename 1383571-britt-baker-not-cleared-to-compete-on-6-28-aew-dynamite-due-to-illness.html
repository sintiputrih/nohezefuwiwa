Britt Baker Not Cleared To Compete On 6/28 AEW Dynamite Due To Illness

Publish date: 2024-05-03

Britt Baker has pulled from the June 28 episode of AEW Dynamite.

Shortly after the show started, AEW CEO Tony Khan announced that Baker was not cleared to wrestle due to illness. As a result, her scheduled Owen Hart Foundation Tournament Quarterfinal Match against Ruby Soho will be postponed to the July 5 episode.

WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available.

AEW Fight Forever Season 3 Revealed, Featuring Swerve Strickland, Claudio Castagnoli, And More

Publish date: 2024-08-23

All Elite Wrestling continues to produce post-launch DLC content for AEW Fight Forever.

Last week on All Elite Arcade, Fight Forever Season 3 was officially announced. It was then revealed that Swerve Strickland would be the first character released on February 14.

This morning, AEW Games took to social media to finally announce the complete contents of Season 3 of AEW Fight Forever, including:

  • Swerve Strickland
  • Claudio Castagnoli
  • Jamie Hayter
  • Day and Evening Beach Maps
  • 33 brand new moves
  • 42 new skin and attire options
  • 11 all-new music tracks

Three separate packs make up this season pass:

  • Swerve to the Beach (Released today, February 14)
  • Giant Swing in the Ring (Releases March 6, according to Nintendo)
  • Hayter’s Gunna Game (Releases April 10, according to Nintendo)

You can purchase the AEW Fight Forever Season 3 pass now for $16.99 on Xbox, PlayStation, Switch, and PC via Steam.

Complete 12/27 WWE House Show MSG Line-Up: Matches, More

Publish date: 2024-07-13

The following is being advertised for the WWE live event in Madison Square Garden in NYC on 12/27:

-C.M Punk vs. John Cena vs. The Miz vs. Alberto Del Rio in a four-way cage mach for the WWE Championship.

-Jerry Lawler vs. Michael McGullicutty.

-Mason Ryan vs. Drew McIntyre.

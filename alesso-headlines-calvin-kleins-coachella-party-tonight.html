Alesso Headlines Calvin Kleins Coachella Party Tonight

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Recommended Videos

It’s finally here, folks. Coachella is now underway and the excitement in Indio, CA is starting to heat up. Of course, along with the festival itself comes a ton of very hot parties, and while there are plenty of options for people to choose from, we’ve got our eye on the one that Calvin Klein is throwing down later tonight.

Taking place in at a warehouse in Palm Springs, the party will be headlined by none other than Swedish superstar Alesso, who will be getting support from Brodinski, Flat White and more. Additionally, with the company’s massive roster of celebrities who appear in their campaigns, you can bet that there will be a whole host of familiar face roaming around the party.

The whole thing kicks off at 11pm and goes late into the night, giving partygoers a chance to dance their hearts out until the sun comes up. Again, it’s sure to be one of the weekend’s hottest events and if you’re in Palm Springs, we definitely suggest you do your best to check it out.

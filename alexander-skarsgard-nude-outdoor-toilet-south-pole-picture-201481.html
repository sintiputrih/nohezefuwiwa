Alexander Skarsgard Sits Nude on Toilet in South Pole: Picture

Publish date: 2024-04-20

Leave it to Alexander Skarsgard to make sitting naked in below-freezing temps look sizzling hot. The True Blood star — no stranger to nudity — stripped down to nothing but a pair of snow boots and sunglasses after reaching the South Pole in Antarctica in mid-December, and thanks to a pal, the moment was captured on film for posterity.

Inge Solheim, Skarsgard's team guide on the Walking With the Wounded Challenge — the same trek that Prince Harry took last month — shared a snapshot of the actor via Instagram on Tuesday, Jan. 7. The photo shows the 37-year-old hunk naked and sitting on an outdoor toilet while reading a book.

"What are you reading, Alex? The script for season 7 of #truebloodHBO?" Solheim captioned the image, noting the -30 degree Celsius temperature.

Of course, this isn't the first time Skarsgard has made headlines for baring his bod. During the season six finale of True Blood, in a scene very similar to the South Pole picture, the actor lounged fully nude on a chaise lounge chair in snowy Sweden.

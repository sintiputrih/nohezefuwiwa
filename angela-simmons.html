Angela Simmons Biography, Age, Parents & Early Life

Angela Simmons was born on 18th September 1987 in New York City, New York, USA. Her full birth name is Angela Renee Simmons. Furthermore, the age of the famous shoe designer is Thirty (30) years old as of 2018. Her father “Joseph Simmons Sr.” is a famous American music personality. Her mother “Valerie Vaughn” is the first wife of her father.

Moreover, she has two siblings named “Vanessa Simmons” and “Joseph “JoJo” Ward Simmons Jr.”. Angela has step-mother “Justine Simmons” who is the second wife of her father. Moreover, she has half-brothers Russell, and Diggy Simmon. Her half-sisters’ names are Miley Justine Simmons and Late Victoria Anne Simmons (half-sister).

The 30-year-old beautiful designer attended High School of Fashion Industries for her school education. Later, she went to Fashion Institute of Technology for graduation. In 2016, she was engaged to her boyfriend “Sutton Tennyson”. But they parted away due to some problems.

Angela also has a son “Sutton Joseph Tennyson” with her ex-fiance.

Angela Simmons Net worth: $7.5 million US Dollars

In 2005, she started her career by appearing in her family’s show Run’s House. Later, in 2006, she became the editor of her magazine company Angela’s Rundown. It was a fashion magazine specially created for the teenagers.

In 2007, Angela launched her own shoe line called “Pastry Footwear”. She was the co-founder along with her elder sister Vanessa Simmons. As of 2018, the estimated Net worth of Angela Simmons is Approx. $7.5 million USD.

Again, in 2009, she appeared in the reality show “Daddy’s Girls”. Moreover, she has her own fashion accessory line and also e-commerce website. In her career, she also appeared in many other reality shows like “Fashion News Live” (2008-2014), “Growing Up Hip Hop” (2016) and “The Real” (2016).

How did Anna Muzychuk start her Professional Career?

Chess is something Anna started doing when she was 14 years old, in 2004. She was a member of the Slovenian national team up until 2014. She also became the fourth woman to reach the 2600 Elo rating level, achieving it in July 2012 with a score of 2606.

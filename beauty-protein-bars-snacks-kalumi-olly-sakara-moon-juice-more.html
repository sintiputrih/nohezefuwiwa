Beauty, Protein Bars, Snacks: Kalumi, Olly, Sakara, Moon Juice, More

Publish date: 2024-05-30

You’ve heard the sayings a million times: Beauty comes from within. You are what you eat. But, let’s be honest, that 3 p.m. chocolate craving is real and making good eating decisions while at work or on-the-go is not always easy. Fortunately, there is a whole new crop of wellness-oriented bars and snacks that taste great, aren’t overloaded with added sugar and pack a beautifying punch.

Whether you’re looking for something sweet or savory, post-workout or at your desk, there are plenty of options that will leave you feeling (and maybe even looking!) good. Marine collagen-infused “beauty food” bars, for example, can offer an inside-out approach to skincare thanks to nutrient-rich superfoods that have known anti-aging benefits. If savory snacks are more your thing, there are adaptogenic nuts and crisps that are sure to fit your fancy or infused coconut butters that can be enjoyed any which way. And many have a hunger-curbing benefit since fiber- and protein-packed delights will keep you fuller longer. Keep scrolling to see our favorites!

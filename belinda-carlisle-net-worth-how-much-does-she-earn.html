Belinda Carlisle bio

Harold Carlisle, a gas station attendant, and his wife Joanne, a housewife, welcomed their daughter Belinda Jo Carlisle into the world on August 17, 1958 in Hollywood, Los Angeles, California. Carlisle was born nine months after her mother and her father—who was 20 years her senior—met.

She was given the name Johnny Belinda in honour of her mother’s favourite movie, Johnny Belinda (1948). Carlisle’s father left their family when she was five years old, and she has said that the majority of her childhood was spent in poverty. She remembered having “like, two outfits” when she was an adolescent. Carlisle claimed that while her father was not religious, her mother was quite devout.

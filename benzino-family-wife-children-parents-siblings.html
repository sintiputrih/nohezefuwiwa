Benzino family, wife, children, parents, siblings

Publish date: 2024-10-06

Raymond Leon Scott, better known by his stage name Benzino, is an American urban media proprietor, television personality, rapper, and record producer. He produced records and co-owned the magazine The Source.

Table of Content

Benzino wife

Benzino marital life is unclear. He is known to have dated Karlie Redd and Althea Heart. Scott and Heart had a son together in November 2015, but they broke off their engagement shortly thereafter. They appeared together on Season 6 of the television show Marriage Boot Camp chronicling their broken relationship.

Benzino children

Benzino has four children who are Ray Ray ‘Chavo’, Coi Leray, Taj Collins and Zino.

Benzino parents

Benzino was born on July 18, 1965, in Boston, Massachusetts, United States, and raised by his parents Edward DeJesus and Mary Scott. He considered Edward DeJesus to be his father figure, especially in the duration of DeJesus’s incarceration.

Benzino siblings

Benzino has two sisters namely Anita Scott-Wilson and Maureen Scott. His sister Maureen is a medical doctor, and his other sister, Anita Scott-Wilson, has a doctorate in veterinary medicine from the Tuskegee Institute.

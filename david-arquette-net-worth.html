David Arquette | Lifestyle and Vacation

Lifestyle

Actor and wrestler David Arquette lives a luxurious lifestyle with a massive $30 million net worth.

He was born to Brenda “Mardi” Olivia, an actress and poet, and Lewis Arquette, an actor.

Arquette’s mother was Jewish, and his father was a Muslim convert from Catholicism.

Arquette and his siblings had a difficult upbringing because their father was an alcoholic, and their mother was frequently harsh. Their mom passed away from breast cancer in 1997.

Regardless of the difficulty, all four of his siblings, Rosanna, Richmond, Patricia, and Alexis, became actors.

On the other hand, Alexis died in 2016 after suffering a heart attack following decades of living with HIV.

David had a vicious streak as a kid. He started shoplifting and using alcohol when he was just 12 years old.

His family had moved twice by the time he was a teenager, once to Chicago and then to Los Angeles, where he attended the Los Angeles Center for Enriched Studies.

Following graduation, Arquette pursued a career in television and film.

On June 12, 1999, Arquette got married to actress Courteney Cox.

Coco Arquette, their daughter, was born in 2004. Jennifer Aniston, another well-known actress, is Coco’s godmother.

After roughly two years of separation with Cox, David filed for divorce in June 2012, and the divorce was finalized in 2013.

In 2011, he began dating Christina McLarty, a correspondent for Entertainment Tonight.

Moreover, in 2013, the couple revealed that they had their first child together, a baby boy.

In 2014, the pair confirmed their engagement, and they tied the knot in 2015.

In 2017, David and his wife had their second child.

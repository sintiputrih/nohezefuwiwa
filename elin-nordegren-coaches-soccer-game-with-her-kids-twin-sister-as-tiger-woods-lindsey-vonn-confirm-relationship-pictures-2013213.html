Publish date: 2024-07-06

Behold some very, very good genes. On Wednesday, March 20, Elin Nordegren displayed a lesser-known talent — soccer coaching! — as she hit the field at a kids’ game in Jupiter, Fla. where daughter Sam, 5, and son Charlie, 3, both appeared to be playing. Also along for the athletic outing: Josefin, the former model’s twin sister, and Josefin’s own little boy.

Smiling and cheering on the kids, 33-year-old Elin wore a baseball cap with her blonde hair hanging loose, a jersey, sweatpants and sneakers. Her stunning doppelganger sister, a London-based attorney, wore shorts and a blue sweatshirt, with her hair pulled back as she watched the game from the stands with her blond little boy in her lap. Not present for the outing: Chris Cline, Elin’s rumored new boyfriend, a 53-year-old coal billionaire and a neighbor who lives near Elin’s massive $12 million oceanfront property in North Palm Beach, Fla.

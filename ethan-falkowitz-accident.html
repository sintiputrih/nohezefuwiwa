The accident happened on North Broadway, and Amandeep Singh, 34, was driving a Dodge Ram southbound in the northbound lanes when he crashed into a sedan carrying the four teenagers. Two of the boys, Drew Hassenbein and Ethan Falkowitz, both 14, were killed instantly, while the other two were hospitalized with internal injuries. Hassenbein was a nationally ranked and highly talented tennis player, while Falkowitz was also a star player on the local high school varsity team. The four boys were reportedly returning home after celebrating their victory in a tennis competition.

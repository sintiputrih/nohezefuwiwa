FIFA Club World Cup Final: Prize money for Man City, Fluminense, Al Ahly revealed

Publish date: 2024-07-29

The 2023 FIFA Club World Cup final saw Manchester City emerge as champions, earning a prize money of $5 million, while Fluminense, the runners-up, will receive $4 million. Additionally, Al Ahly, who secured third place, will be awarded $2.5 million. The tournament took place at King Abdullah Sports City in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. In the final, Manchester City defeated Fluminense with a score of 4-0, marking their first Club World Cup win. The goals for Manchester City were scored by Julián Álvarez and Phil Foden. Earlier, Al Ahly triumphed over Urawa Red Diamonds, securing the third-place position and earning the prize money.

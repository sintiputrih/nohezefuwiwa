Georgina Rodriguez Biography

Publish date: 2024-03-03

QUICK FACTS
Nationalities Argentina, Spain
Birth January 27, 1994 (Buenos Aires, Argentina)

BIOGRAPHY
Georgina Rodríguez Hernández is a model, businesswoman and influencer, born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, who became known when she became the partner of the famous soccer player Cristiano Ronaldo. In 2017, she began a career as a model, becoming the cover of various magazines such as Vogue , La Gazzetta, Grazia, Men’s Health, Glamorous, among others. In addition to being the ambassador of luxury brands, which led her to become the most followed Spanish model on the social network Instagram with more than 27 million followers, becoming a phenomenon. In 2020, she debuted as a presenter at the Sanremo Song Festival. In October of that year, she participated in the Antena 3 program Mask Singer: guess who sings , where she played the role of León performing the song ‘Si por mi fue’ by Beret. In 2021, she debuted as an entrepreneur when she launched her own brand ‘OM By G’

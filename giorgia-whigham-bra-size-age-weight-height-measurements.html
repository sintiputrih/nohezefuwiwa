Biography:

Giorgia Whigham was born on August 18, 1997. Her father is actor Shea Whigham. In 2016, she made her acting debut appearing in an episode of Shameless. She next appeared as Shannon in some episodes of Son of Zorn. She next acted in the TV movie The Legend of Master Legend, as well as played Kat in 2 episodes of 13 Reasons Why.

After this, she made one-episode roles in several shows, including Chance, The Orville, Animal Kingdom, and Dirty John. She also played a supporting role in the Netflix movie Sierra Burgess Is a Loser. Her first starring role on the small screen was as Amy Bendix in Netflix’s The Punisher. She simultaneously starred as Beth in Season 3 of Scream. Her latest film role was in 2019’s Saving Zoë.

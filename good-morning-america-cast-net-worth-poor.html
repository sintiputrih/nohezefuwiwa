These Are The Poorest 'Good Morning America' Cast Members

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Highlights

  • Good Morning Americ a's poorest cast members have net worths between $1-2.5 million, with annual salaries ranging from $61,829 to $500,000.
  • Janai Norman, Whit Johnson, and Marysol Castro are among the lower earners on the show, with net worths and salaries below those of the top hosts.
  • As of early 2024, Good Morning America still holds the top spot in morning news shows, leading to potential financial growth for its cast if trends continue.

Good Morning America (also well-known as GMA) airs between 7:00 am and 9:00 am on the ABC network. The show has been on the air since November 1975, starting off with being on weekdays only before bringing in the weekend airings in 1993.

While some cast members would be considered the richest Good Morning America host, others have not been so lucky and are in fact, rather poor considering being on the morning show. For example, since leaving his former talk show, Live! With Kelly And Michael, Michael Strahan has become the richest cast member on the show with a net worth of $65 million and a salary of approximately $17 million.

Robin Roberts and George Stephanopoulos are also considered to be quite rich. Robin has a net worth of $45 million and a salary of around $18 million. Whereas George has a net worth of $40 million and approximately a salary of $15 million.

Today, we are breaking down the poorest cast members on Good Morning America.

8 Ginger Zee

Net Worth: $2.5 Million

  • Zee's salary is $500,000

First up on the list is Ginger Zee. Ginger is the current co-host and chief meteorologist. She started off as the weather anchor on Good Morning America on the weekend shows in 2011, before being moved to the weekday airings in 2013.

When she is not on the air broadcasting the weather for her viewers, Ginger has taken an interest in writing. She has recently released her memoir 'A Little Closer To Home' which is about her overcoming and healing from mental health.

Ginger has a net worth of $2.5 million and her salary is approximately $500,000 per year.

7 Rob Marciano

Net Worth: $2 Million

  • Marciano's salary is $76,700

Second on the list is Rob Marciano, who is the current senior meteorologist on the weekend airings. He has been working on Good Morning America since 2014.

Before joining the cast of Good Morning America, Rob was a co-anchor on Entertainment Tonight in 2013 and was CNN's American Morning weather anchor from 2003 until 2011.

Rob has a net worth of $2 million and a reported salary of around $76,700 per year.

6 Dan Harris

Net Worth: $1.5 Million

  • Harris' salary was $99,712

Thirdly on the list is a former anchor on the weekend edition of the morning show, Dan Harris.

Dan Harris was on Good Morning America between the years 2010 and 2021.

Dan has since retired from journalism and has put his focus on his company, Ten Percent Happier, which involves multiple podcasts and information regarding mental health struggles and mindfulness.

Dan Harris has a net worth of $1.5 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth and had a salary of around $99,712.

5 Linsey Davis

Net Worth: $1.5 Million

  • Davis' salary is $78,569

Next up on the list is Linsey Davis. She is the newest addition to the Good Morning America family.

In 2021, Linsey was brought onto the cast as an anchor for the weekend airings, replacing Dan Harris.

Off the air, Linsey is using her free time writing children's books. In March 2021, she released a new children's book called Stay This Way Forever, which she calls a "love letter from parents to their children."

Since being on the show, Linsey has gained a net worth of $1.5 million and her salary is around $78,569.

She has also started to make headlines by standing up for the news industry standards by embracing her simple and basic hairstyles that suit her, leaving her hair natural while on the air.

Janai has a net worth of $1 million and an approximate salary of $100,000 per year.

3 Whit Johnson

Net Worth: $1 Million

  • Johnson's salary is $87,435

Going a little further down on the list is Whit Johnson. Whit has been on Good Morning America’s weekend edition as an anchor since 2018.

While with KNBC, Whit had the opportunity to report on the 2014 Winter Olympic Games in Sochi, Russia and the 2016 Summer Olympic Games in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil (including the Emmy-winning Special Report of the Opening Ceremony).

Whit has a net worth of $1 million and a salary of around $87,435.

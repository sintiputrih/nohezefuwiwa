About myHISD

MyHISD is an online resource for the Houston Independent School District’s staff. Employees can quickly obtain all the relevant information through this portal from a single webpage.

Employees can view the latest news, access several online tools, and remotely cooperate with other departments once they have signed into the myHISD login page.

For employees in Houston, the HISD Login or HISD Employee Portal is quite helpful. It gathers all the crucial information in one place and simplifies communication with upper management and coworkers.

Now that you have a basic understanding of the myHISD login page let’s move on to understanding myHISD Login. But first, you need to be aware of the myHISD login criteria.

Houston ISD Login Requirements

You must meet a few prerequisites to access the Houston ISD login page. The following items are required for you to access your employee portal:

  • The login page’s official web URL for Houston ISD.
  • Login Information for myHISD: Username and Password.
  • Computer/Smartphone/Tablet.
  • dependable online access.
  • Modernised web browser.

How to Login to Houston Isd Portal?

Please follow the instructions below to log in as a Houston ISD employee.

  • Visit the official website at https://www.houstonisd.org/myhisd

  • Then click the login button.

  • Now you must provide your MyHISD username, username@domain, followed by your password.
  • Use username@houstonISD.org for employees.
  • Fill in the blank space with username@online.houstonisd.org for students.

Press next when you have successfully provided your login information to access your account.

How to Reset Houston ISD Login Account?

You must reset your myHISD login password if you’ve lost or forgotten it. By following the straightforward procedures listed below, you can reset the password for your Houston ISD employee account:

  • You must first click on this link: houstonisd.org/myhisd.
  • Select the Login to myHISD option under the myHISD option.
  • Click the Can’t access your account? Link after that.

  • Next, choose a professional or personal account, then a username, and finally, complete the captcha.
  • Next, select the Next button.
  • Follow the on-screen directions to reset your Houston ISD login password from there.

FAQs

Whats Employee orientation?

Every new employee joining Houston is allowed to participate in this programme. For more information, please visit https://www.houstonisd.org/newemployeeorientation.

How does the Microsoft team work?

For further information, please visit https://www.houstonisd.org/Page/188986. Microsoft Team is a virtual platform used to organise meetings and provide training to employees.

How many paid leaves does an employee get at Houston?

It would help if you spoke with the appropriate department manager to learn more about the paid leave details.

How often do HISD teachers get paid?

Every year, there are 26 pay periods, and every HISD employee receives biweekly compensation. Monday through Sunday make up this pay period.

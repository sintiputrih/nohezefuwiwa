Who is Jane Fallon?

Jane Fallon is an English author. She has written eight top ten best-selling novels to date including ‘Getting Rid of Matthew’, ‘Got You Back’, and ‘Foursome’.

Additionally, she is the wife of popular actor and comedian Ricky Gervais.

How many siblings does Jane Fallon have?

Fallon was born in Harrow, London on December 9, 1960. Her parents had a newsagent’s shop. She spent the majority of her childhood years in London’s Harrow neighborhood.

She is of British nationality. Furthermore, her ethnic background is mixed(Irish, German, and Norwegian).

Talking about her education, Fallon attended St Bernard’s Convent School in Slough. Additionally, she studied history at the University College London.

Fallon has worked as a producer in several TV series. From 1994 to 1995, she was a producer for ‘EastEnders’. She was also the producer of another TV series ‘This Life’ from 1996 to 1997.

Furthermore, she has worked as a producer on shows including ‘Undercover Heart’, ‘Massive Landmarks of the 20th Century’, ‘20 Things to Do Before You’re 30’, ‘Single’, and ‘Teachers’.

Additionally, she was also the writer for ‘20 Things to Do Before You’re 30’ and ‘Jupiter Moon’. Apart from her work on television, Fallon is also a successful author.

She has written books including ‘Got You Back’, ‘The Ugly Sister’, ‘Skeletons’, ‘Strictly Between Us’, ‘My Sweet Revenge’, and ‘Faking Friends. The latter of which is her most recent work. Furthermore, her novels have been translated into nearly two dozen languages to date.

Fallon has received four BAFTA TV Award nominations. She received the award in the years 1997, 1998, 1999, and 2003.

Jharrel Jerome (born October 09, 1997) is an American Actor and Instagram Personality from Bronx, New York. He recently gained huge success for playing the role of Korey Wise in Netflix miniseries called “When They See Us“.

Wise was only 16-year-old when he was wrongfully convicted, suffered the longest and harshest sentence. Furthermore, the show made of four episodes which aired on the 31st of May 2019.

Ava DuVernay is the director, creator, and writer of the hit series. Moreover, it is based on a true story which portrays the life of all five Afro-American teenagers who got convicted of a rape they never commit.

Raymond Santana, Antron McCray, Kevin Richardson, Yusef Salaam, and Korey Wise falsely sentenced of raping and attempted murder of Trisha Meili (a white female jogger) in the Central Park Jogger Case.

Jharrel Jerome started his career in the film “Moonlight” in 2016. He played the role of Kevin (age 16). Later, the actor portrayed one of the recurring roles in the drama series titled “Mr. Mercedes” as Jerome Robinson.

In addition, he also played the main character in the short film “Robu” as Rob. As an Instagram personality, Jerome has a decent fan-following on the platform with the 117k+ followers.

Early Life, Parents & Profile

The actor was born on the 9th of October 1997 in Bronx, New York, United States. Therefore, Jharrel Jerome’s age is 21 years old, as of 2019. His mother name is Radoyka Dee Jerome but there is no information about his father on the internet.

Furthermore, he belongs to multiracial ethnicity and is of Afro-Dominican descent. As of education, he completed his high school from Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts.

Jerome found himself a classmate of Ansel Elgort. They would go to auditions with no agent or manager. But talent explorers usually came to the school. They eventually offered Jharrel opportunities after seeing his theater performances. Later, he graduated from Ithaca College in 2019.

The actor received a Bachelor of Fine Arts in acting. During college, he auditioned and landed a role in Moonlight. The movie became a huge hit and also received various Oscar nominations.

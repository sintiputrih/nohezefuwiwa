Who is Josh Jacobs?

For those unfamiliar, Joshua Jacobs, born on February 11, 1998, is an American football running back for the Las Vegas Raiders in the NFL. Josh played college football at Alabama. He was selected in the first round of NFL Draft 2019 by the Raiders.

Apart from his football career, Jacobs’ younger brother, Isaiah Jacobs, is a running back for the UAB Blazers. Jacobs has Filipino descent through his paternal grandmother from Angeles City, and he has a tattoo of her portrait on his left hand.

Raised in Tulsa, Oklahoma, Jacobs attended McLain High School, where he had an impressive high school football career with 5,372 yards and 56 touchdowns for the Titans. Despite facing homelessness in middle school, Jacobs overcame adversity and reflected on his experience in a Kia commercial aired during Super Bowl LIV at the end of his rookie NFL season.

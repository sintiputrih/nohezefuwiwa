Justin Langer Biography

Justin Langer was born on November 21, 1970, in Perth, Western Australia, Australia. He played for the Australian National Cricket Team from 1993 to 2007 and is a former cricket coach and player from Australia. From March 5, 2018, until April 2, 2022, he also served as the Australian Men’s National Cricket Team’s coach.

He was born to Colin Langer, a car salesman, and Joy-Anne Langer, a nurse. In July 2017, his mother passed away at the age of 68. He has two brothers, Jonathan Langer, and Adam Langer, as well as a sister called Jemma. He wed Sue on April 13, 1996. The couple was blessed with four daughters: Ali-Rose, Grace, Jessica, and Sophie. His uncle is the late Australian cricketer Robert Samuel Langer (Rob Langer).

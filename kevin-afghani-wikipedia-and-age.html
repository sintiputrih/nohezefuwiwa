Kevin Afghani Wikipedia , Who is Kevin Afghani?

Kevin is a renowned American voice actor who is best known at the moment for his role in being the current voice actor for Mario and Luigias of 2023, beginning with Super Mario Bros. Wonder.

Born as Kevin Zachary Afghani, he grew up having the decision to become a voice actor for a single reason according to hom. He made that reason known as his absolute love for animation and video games.

Kevin made it known to the general public vis his website that, animation and video games are undoubtedly some of the most genuinely exciting forms of art that I have ever seen. Characters that exist only within the context of their media allow this art to transform into a beautifully immersive experience like no other.

Kevin went on and stated clearly that whether that experience is fun, bubbly, dark, or moving, this art form is truly amazing and he absolutely had to become a part of it.
​
While describing just how much passion he had for his profession, Kevin Afghani stated emphatically that what he consider as Characters are made up of a lot of things, but passion is what makes them truly exist being it a writers passion, a directors passion, an actors passion, and so many more. He just wanted to to lend his passion to whatever characters he can and also intended to give a piece of himself to the things that he love and that is really it.

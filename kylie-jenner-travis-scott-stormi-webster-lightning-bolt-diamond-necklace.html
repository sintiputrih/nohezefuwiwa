Kylie Jenner &amp; Travis Scott Gift Stormi Webster a Diamond Lightning Bolt Necklace

Publish date: 2024-09-23
  • Following her extra special first birthday party, which included the creation of theme park #StormiWorld in her honor, baby Stormi was gifted a glitzy diamond chain by her father, Travis Scott.
  • Mom Kylie Jenner shared video on Instagram of her daughter's sparkly new gift, which features a lightning bolt design.

It already goes without saying that one-year-old Stormi Webster has better accessories than most of us. From her baby Louis Vuitton purse, to her first Chanel bag, Stormi's life is pretty glamorous, and it's only getting better by the day.

This weekend, Stormi's mom Kylie Jenner revealed that Travis Scott had purchased something extra special for their baby girl. Sharing the sweetest video of Stormi running around the house, Kylie revealed that her daughter was sporting a brand new diamond necklace. Kylie can be heard in the video exclaiming, "Look at what your daddy got you girl!":

A photo of Stormi on a playdate only further showed how beautiful the one-year-old's necklace is. Featuring a lightning bolt, referring to Stormi's weather-related name, the iconic piece of jewelry is completely individual, and will fill you with envy:

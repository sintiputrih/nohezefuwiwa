Publish date: 2024-05-24

Biography:

Lenora Crichlow was born on January 4, 1985 in Westminster, London, England. She made her feature film debut in Wilderness, playing the role of Mandy, followed by the short film The Beloved Ones.

Her first television appearance was as Shirley Moss in the television series The Bill. She then became known for her role as Maria “Sugar” Sweet in Sugar Rush and as Annie Sawyer in Being Human.

In 2012, she starred as Shania Andrews in the film Fast Girls. That same year, she appeared in the made-for-television film Doors Open, playing the role of Laura Stanton.

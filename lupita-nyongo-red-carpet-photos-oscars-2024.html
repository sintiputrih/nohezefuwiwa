Lupita Nyongo Is Dreamy in a Powder-Blue Gown Covered in Crystals at the 2024 Oscars

Publish date: 2024-03-04

Lupita Nyong’o is back at the Oscars, and she made her return with a bang.

The Little Monsters star glided across the red carpet tonight at Los Angeles’s Dolby Theatre, looking dreamy in a custom Giorgio Armani Privé gown. The beautiful silk-embroidered centerpiece featured a plunging V neckline, crystal bead embellishments throughout, and pale blue feathers at the peplum waist and hem, all in a lovely powder blue. She paired the frock with layers of diamonds, in the form of a stack of dainty bracelets, a ring decorated with two diamond studs, and diamond drop earrings, all from De Beers. As for glam, Nyong’o looked radiant in glowy makeup complete with a berry lip and shimmery eye shadow.

Mike Coppola//Getty Images

Although Nyong’o is not up for a nomination tonight, she is among an A-list group of presenters. The star-studded roster includes the likes of Zendaya, Michelle Yeoh, Emily Blunt, America Ferrera, Ryan Gosling, Ariana Grande, Chris Hemsworth, Jennifer Lawrence, Matthew McConaughey, Al Pacino, Octavia Spencer, Anya Taylor-Joy, and many others.

The Black Panther actor won an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress in 2014, for her performance in 12 Years a Slave. Over the years, Nyong’o has opted in and out of the ceremony, attending in 2015, 2018, 2019, and 2022. And every time she does step out for Hollywood’s biggest night, she brings her sartorial best. In her last appearance, at the 94th Academy Awards, she looked utterly breathtaking in a yellow-gold sequined Prada gown adorned with dazzling pink floral embellishments and gilded streamer details.

picture alliance//Getty Images

While Nyong’o has skipped most of the award shows on this year’s circuit, she did show off her glamorous red-carpet style last month at the 74th Berlin International Film Festival, where she served as president of the international jury. At the time, she looked gorgeous in a black Boss gown that featured a thigh-high slit, an asymmetrical halter neckline, and shiny gold and silver sequins and beads that formed illustrative, whimsical patterns all over.

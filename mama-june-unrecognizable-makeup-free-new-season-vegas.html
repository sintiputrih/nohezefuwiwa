Mama June looks unrecognizable in makeup-free selfie and teases new reality show season after Vegas

Publish date: 2024-07-22

MAMA June was looking quite unrecognizable in a makeup-free selfie as she teased a new season of her reality show.

All this comes after the TV star was spotted in Las Vegas with her boy toy, TikToker Jordan McCollum.

The 42-year-old took to her Instagram Stories to post the makeup-free photo, which features her smiling while sporting wet hair.

She wrote over the snap: "Headed to Georgia to do a lil filming. Can't wait for y'all to see what is going on with the family this upcoming season."

The family's series, Mama June: From Not to Hot, debuted back in 2017 and is a spin-off of the popular TLC show Here Comes Honey Boo Boo.

There is no word yet if June's new man Jordan, 24, will make an appearance, despite the duo being seen together in Las Vegas recently.

In The Sun’s exclusive photos, the couple were seen exiting a stretch limousine at their luxury, VIP hotel accommodations on the strip in Sin City.

Previously, the TikToker bragged about the getaway he was about to embark on, as he told his followers he was flying from Alabama, to Atlanta and then onto Vegas.

Jordan was even able to win $2,600 at a slot machine on their first night in town.

Before their trip, June told one of the social media star's family members about their travel plans on her social media.

She commented on Facebook: “I can’t wait to see his reaction as we fly into the city at night and just to see his reaction overall while we are there but I think we deserve it just to be able to get away."

ANNA'S CONCERNS

However, not everyone is about June's new man, as her oldest daughter, Anna Cardwell, says she is concerned with her mom’s relationship.

Jordan has detailed his struggles with sobriety on TikTok.

She exclusively told The Sun: "If he starts using again, that's not healthy for Mama and he’s only 24-years-old. Mama needs to calm down and focus on herself."

Anna added: “He doesn't have a lot of time on him. If he relapses, I hope he at least does it away from her. She’s on probation, and if she fails a drug test at any time she will go back to jail."

JUNE'S TROUBLES

In 2019, the mother of four was arrested on drug charges, including possession of a controlled substance and drug paraphernalia charges in Alabama.

She was arrested with her then-boyfriend Geno Doak, who was charged with third-degree domestic violence, possession of a controlled substance, and possession of drug paraphernalia.

Geno, 45, was sentenced to 16 months behind bars, while June avoided any jail time and received community service instead.

June's daughter Lauryn "Pumpkin" Shannon, 21, is currently looking over her sister Alana "Honey Boo Boo" Thompson.

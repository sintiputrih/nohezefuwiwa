Marilou York (Mark Hamill Wife) Wiki, Bio, Age, Husband, Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Marilou York is a celebrity spouse and a dental hygienist from the United States. She is most known for being the wife of Star Wars actor Mark Hamill. In 1978, the couple married. Mark Hamill is also an American actor, voice actor, and writer. He is most recognized for his portrayal of Luke Skywalker in the Star Wars film trilogy, for which he received three Saturn Awards. Corvette Summer (1978) and The Big Red One (1980) are two of his previous cinematic performances. Hamill has also appeared on stage in a number of theater productions, mostly in the 1980s.

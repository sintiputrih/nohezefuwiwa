Actor best known for his role as Bilbo Baggins in Peter Jackson's The Hobbit films. He has also starred in The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy and in the 2003 film Love Actually. In 2014, he began playing Lester Nygaard on the FX series Fargo. In 2018, he appeared in Marvel's Black Panther.

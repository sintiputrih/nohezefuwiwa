5 Minka Kelly's Early Career In Acting

Minka Kelly left Friday Night Lights because she felt as if her character needed to move on. The storyline is focused on high school characters and Lyla was off to college! After she left Dillon, Texas to study at Vanderbilt, the show went on for two more seasons.

Minka Kelly then appeared on Parenthood as Max's behavioral aid that ends up having an affair with Crosby. Kelly also appears briefly as Autumn in (500) Days Of Summer and also had a cameo in Just Go With It. Kelly starred as Sara in the thriller The Roommate alongside Leighton Meester, and she was also in the movies The Butler, Away and Back, Naked, The Beach House, and Lansky.

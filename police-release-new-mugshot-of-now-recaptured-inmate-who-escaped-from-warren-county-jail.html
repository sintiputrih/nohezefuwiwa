Police release new mugshot of now recaptured inmate who escaped from Warren County jail

Publish date: 2024-04-23

Police in Pennsylvania have released a new mugshot of recaptured prisoner Michael Burham.

Burham was arrested Saturday in Pennsylvania after escaping from the Warren County Jail more than a week earlier.

He is a suspect in the killing of a woman in New York and is accused of abducting an older couple in Pennsylvania.

Burham was on the run for ten days after using a rope made from bed sheets to escape from jail on July 6th.

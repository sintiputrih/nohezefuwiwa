Rihanna Is All of Us Watching Kendrick Lamar at Coachella

Publish date: 2024-08-03

Rihanna showed off a killer wardrobe at Coachella this weekend, but she also came to wild out to some music. In a new video taken by a Coachella attendee over the weekend, the singer was caught jamming out to Kendrick Lamar's headlining set with the rest of the crowd. She wasn't afraid to jump up and down, belt out the lyrics to "Alright" and even let out a little stank face for the camera.

BadGal RiRi was among the crowd of eager fans who watched Lamar perform his new album DAMN. live for the first time. But Rihanna had an extra reason to be excited; she collaborated with the rapper for a track on the new record.

About

6'7" NBA guard who was drafted 4th overall by the Los Angeles Clippers straight out of high school in 2004. After suffering a career-threatening injury in 2007 where he tore his ACL, PCL, and MCL while dislocating numerous bones, he made a remarkable comeback to the NBA and helped the Golden State Warriors win NBA Championships in 2015 and 2017. 

Before Fame

He won back-to-back Class AA Illinois State Titles with Peoria Central High School in 2003 and 2004.

Trivia

After his devastating injury, he bounced around the NBA, playing for the Miami Heat, Oklahoma City Thunder, Washington Wizards, Charlotte Bobcats, Milwaukee Bucks, Cleveland Cavaliers and Brooklyn Nets before signing with the Warriors in 2014.

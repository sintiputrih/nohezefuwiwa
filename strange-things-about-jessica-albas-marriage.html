Strange Things About Jessica Alba's Marriage

Publish date: 2024-08-21

A year after their fateful first encounter on the set of the first Fantastic Four film, Jessica Alba gushed about her new leading man. "Yes, his name is Cash. ... he's not famous. He might be The One," she told Cosmopolitan. Well, okay then! Jessica Alba: actress, businesswoman, and fortune teller! 

They weren't able to embark on a whirlwind romance right away, though. "It was kind of a love-at-first-sight thing, but I met him when I was dating someone else, so it started off as a friendship," she said. Ruh roh.

Despite being involved with another man, Alba said she was "attracted to [Warren's] spirit," and, when they finally became an item, he sealed the deal in the corniest way. "He gave me a note and signed it with a dollar sign, because his name is Cash. It just said, 'I really, really like you.' And that was exactly how I felt at the time. Really, really liking him too much ... I was already in love with him."

This is a cute love story, as long as you're not the guy Alba dumped for Warren!

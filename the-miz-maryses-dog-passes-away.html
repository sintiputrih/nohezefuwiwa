The Miz &amp; Maryses Dog Passes Away

Publish date: 2024-07-11

The Miz and Maryse’s family has been highlighted by their reality television show. Fans got to know their dogs Luna, Pumpkin, and Mocha well. Now Mocha is sadly no longer with us.

The A-Lister posted an Instagram message to inform fans of the sad fact that Mocha died. They will miss her so much and he posted a very touching post to remember everything that they’ve been through as well.

Mocha, we are gonna miss you so much. We will miss you greeting us at the door, nudging our hand to pet you, jumping on our freshly made bed throwing the pillows off and laying on it staking your claim, seeing you in your spot, you never letting us win a game of tug, always protecting us, your warmth, your sweetness, your big kisses.

Even though you are gone your memory lives on and the love we have in our hearts will never go away. We love you. Rest in piece our sweet beautiful Mocha.

Our thoughts are with Miz and Maryse during this time. No cause of death was mentioned, but Mocha lived an incredible life with the It Family. Now they will have to carry on without her. This is a very sad day and we feel for them because it’s never easy losing a pet.

Tia Mowry Wiki Biography

Tia Dashon Mowry-Hardrict, commonly known as Tia Mowry, is a famous American voice actress, television producer, singer, as well as an actress. To the public, Tia Mowry is perhaps best known for her appearance in the sitcom called “Sister, Sister”, in which she appeared alongside Tamera Mowry, her twin sister. The show, created by Kim Bass, Gary Gilbert and Fred Shafferman, first aired on television screens in 1994, and produced a total of six seasons and 119 episodes. With the main focus on the lives of the twin sisters, “Sister, Sister” enjoyed a decent amount of public attention, and ranked at #33 according to Nielsen ratings. Aside from the main cast, the show also featured guest appearances from Lisa Leslie, Kyla Pratt, William Allen Young, Christina Millian and Phill Lewis among others. The show has also been rewarded with numerous Image Awards, Kids’ Choice Awards, and Young Artist Awards.

Prior to their acting success, both sisters were member of a group called “Voices”, which was managed by their mother. Tia Mowry’s career started when she was 12 years old and moved with her parents to California in order to look for acting jobs. Initially, both sisters made on-screen appearances on various television advertisements and commercials.

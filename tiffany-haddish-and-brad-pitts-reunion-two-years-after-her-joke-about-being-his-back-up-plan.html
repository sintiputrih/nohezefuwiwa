Photos of Tiffany Haddish and Brad Pitt posing together emerged from the Vanity Fair Oscar Party in Beverly Hills, where they reunited on Sunday evening.

This reunion took place two years after the comedian made a lighthearted offer to be Brad Pitt’s ‘back-up plan’ following his 2016 split from Angelina Jolie.

During a previous encounter in 2018, she shared that Brad jokingly mentioned the possibility of dating her if they remained single a year later. 

Her humorous revelation came when she was asked to choose between Brad Pitt, George Clooney, and Javier Bardem during an Oscar backstage game of Marry, Date, Ditch with Kelly Ripa.

She recounted: ‘Oh I just met [Brad] in the elevator! He said in one year, if I’m single and he’s single, we’re gonna do it. So you know what that means!’

Tiffany also quipped, ‘But he do got seven kids so I don’t know. I don’t know if I can deal with a man that’s got that many kids.’

When asked why they have to wait a year before dating, Tiffany responded: ‘I don’t know, he told me to wait a year.’

Completing her game, the comedienne disclosed: ‘Okay. So, I would date Brad Pitt, marry George Clooney, and I would ditch Javier, because I don’t know who Javier is.’

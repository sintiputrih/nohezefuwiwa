Trisha Yearwood and Amy Grant Tapped to Host 14th Annual CMA Country Christmas Celeb Secrets Cou

Publish date: 2024-10-06

CMA Country Christmas is back and better than ever!

The fourteenth annual TV special — which is set to air in December — will hosted by Amy Grant and Trisha Yearwood and will feature performances from Ashley McBryde, Jon Pardi, Jordan Davis, The War and Treaty, Lainey Wilson, Lady A, Grant and Yearwood.

Produced by Robert Deaton, directed by Milton Sneed and written by Jon Macks, the show features our favorite country music stars singing holiday classics, with special appearances from a few guests from other genres, including violinist Lindsey Stirling and Christian rock artist Zach Williams.

Filmed in Nashville in front of a live audience, CMA has partnered with Balsam Hill, who provided their beautifully realistic artificial Christmas trees and holiday décor to decorate the “CMA Country Christmas” stage again this year.

CMA Country Christmas airs Thursday, December 14th on ABC at 8pmET and will also be showed on Hulu and Disney+ the next day.

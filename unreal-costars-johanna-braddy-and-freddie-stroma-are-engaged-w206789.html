'UnREAL' Costars Johanna Braddy and Freddie Stroma Are Engaged

Publish date: 2024-09-03

Adam and Anna are engaged! UnREAL costars Johanna Braddy and Freddie Stroma are ready to walk down the aisle, the actor’s rep confirms to Us Weekly.

Stroma, 29, popped the question in Vancouver on Sunday, May 15.

The British star and Braddy, 28, have been dating since meeting on the Lifetime series last year. The pair played reality-TV prince charming (well, sort of) Adam Cromwell and sweet contestant Anna Martin on the addicting show, which (brilliantly!) parodies The Bachelor.

The lovebirds first confirmed their romance during a cast Periscope in August 2015. Since then, Braddy has shared several photos of her now husband-to-be on social media. Back in January, she called Stroma “the most kindhearted and talented man” while wishing him happy birthday.

Up next, the pair will continue working on ABC shows. (The actors won’t be returning to UnREAL.) Braddy currently stars as Shelby Wyatt on the FBI drama Quantico, which was recently renewed for a second season. The Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince hunk, meanwhile, just landed a new role in the Kevin Williamson–created show Time After Time. He will also appear on this season of Game of Thrones as Dickon Tarly.

Was Martin Kemp married to Pepsi?

Publish date: 2024-02-06

Martin Kemp was not married to Pepsi. He is married to Shirlie Holliman, who is an English singer and former backing vocalist of the group Wham! and one half of the 1980s pop duo, Pepsi & Shirlie. They got married in St. Lucia in 1988.

In January 2023, Martin Kemp appeared alongside his wife Shirlie Kemp on the fourth series of The Masked Singer as “Cat & Mouse”. On 24 March 2021, Kemp and his son Roman were featured in an episode of ITV’s DNA Journey.

In 2008, Martin Kemp appeared on the Discovery Channel television programmme Chop Shop where the cast built him a gangster car. From 2004 to 2007, Martin Kemp was the face of the furniture chain ScS.

In December 2017, Martin Kemp appeared as Captain Hook in Peter Pan – The World’s Biggest Pantomime at the Barclaycard Arena in Birmingham and the SSE Arena in Wembley.

In November 2019, Kemp and his wife released their first album together as Martin & Shirlie, titled In the Swing of It via Sony Music. In February 2021, Kemp appeared in McDonald & Dodds.

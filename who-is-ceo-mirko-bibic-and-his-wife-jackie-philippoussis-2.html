Who Is CEO Mirko Bibic and His Wife Jackie Philippoussis?

Publish date: 2024-01-11

Know about Bell CEO Mirko Bibic’s Wikipedia and other details as he defends his decision to remove Lisa LaFlamme.

The head of the BCE Bell, Mirko Bibic, has finally spoken his side of the story after weeks of controversy regarding the removal of LaFlamme.

Bibic decided to part ways with Lisa LaFlamme around a week ago and fired her from the position of chief anchor and senior editor after she spent 35 years with the media company. If you are unaware of the background, you can read about Lisa’s firing, which we have already reported.

Since she was a long-term employee who spent decades in the company, speculations began to circulate that the removal might have been due to her growing age or other personal factors. However, against the talks, Mirko clarified that the decision to remove her was not due to her age, gender, or grey hair, in his Linkedin post.

This response has brought the CEO into the limelight, and here is everything you need to know about him.

Bell CEO Mirko Bibic Wikipedia: Who Is He?

Bell CEO, Mirko Bibic, is a Canadian corporate personality who has been working as the CEO of the Bell media company for over two years.

Although he doesn’t have a dedicated Wikipedia bio page, we can find other details about the man from various social media platforms. He was promoted to Chief Executive Officer of Bell in January 2020 and has worked diligently in that power since the climb.

Before that,  Bibic served as the Chief Operating Officer at BCE Bell for one year and four months. His Linkedin bio further reveals that Mirko worked as EVP, Chief Legal and Regulatory Officer from March 2012 to October 2018, after serving as the Senior Vice President of Regulatory Affairs.

He joined Bell in 2004 and holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree from McGill University and a Law degree from the University of Toronto, as known from his BCE profile.

What Is Bell CEO Mirko Bibic Salary In 2022?

Bell CEO, Mirko Bibic, earns an annual salary of around $4.7 million, per Wallmine.

However, that is not the only money that he takes home in the form of compensation. According to Erieri, Bibic took home a massive amount of $9,477,299 in 2020, which included cash of $2,910,700 and equity of $6 million. On top of that, he also received $566,599 in pension and other forms of compensation.

Similarly, Wallmine further reports that Mirko is the second highest earner at Bell, with Wade Oosterman taking over $6.8 million annually. Based on these numbers, it is clear that Bibic has a great fortune under his control, probably worth multi-millions.

Nonetheless, the official verification is still pending.

Meet Mirko Bibic Wife Jackie Philippoussis

Mirko Bibic has a loving wife in the form of Jackie Philippoussis, who he met during his teenage years.

Although there is little to nothing about Mirko’s wife and other personal details, several online sites mention that Bibic and his spouse, Philippoussis, are parents to three sons. The family currently lives together in Montreal after they moved from Ottawa.

Despite being a top personality, Mirko has a limited online presence and is unavailable on major social media sites. Thus, he likes to keep his family and other aspects private and has never opened up about them publicly.

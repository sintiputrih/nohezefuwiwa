Is Sun Drop going out of business?

CONCORD – Concord-based Sun Drop Bottling Company closed its doors after more than 60 years of business, sold in a deal to Salisbury-based Cheerwine Bottling Co. Sources told the Independent Tribune that the sale went into effect Thursday, Dec. 1.

Who owns Cheerwine and Sun Drop?

Carolina Beverage Corp., the Salisbury maker of Cheerwine, has bought the Sun Drop Bottling Co. of Concord, according to the Independent Tribune newspaper of Concord. Eleven Sun Drop employees were laid off in the process of the sale, which was completed Thursday, writes the Salisbury Post.

Did Cheerwine buy Sun Drop?

SALISBURY, NC (WBTV) – Salisbury-based soft drink provider Cheerwine has purchased the Sun Drop bottling company based in Concord, according to Cheerwine head Cliff Ritchie. According to Ritchie, the formula for Sun Drop will not change. “Dr. Pepper owns the formula,” Ritchie said.

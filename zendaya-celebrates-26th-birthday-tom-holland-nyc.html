Zendaya Celebrates Her 26th Birthday with Boyfriend Tom Holland in NYC

Publish date: 2024-10-05

Zendaya enjoyed a low-key birthday Thursday night.

The Dune actress was spotted enjoying a celebratory dinner at MAMO Italian Restaurant in NYC's SoHo neighborhood, accompanied by her boyfriend Tom Holland, her mother, Claire Stoermer, and her Euphoria co-star Hunter Schafer.

She was photographed looking cool and casual in a black cropped cardigan with long sleeves, which she paired with medium-wash boyfriend jeans and black suede heels. She accessorized the look with dainty stud earrings and a small diamond necklace, with her hair down in loose curls.

Meanwhile, the Spider-Man actor complemented the casual look in a blue plaid button-down, blue corduroy pants and a white pair of Adidas' Superstar sneakers.

The couple also continued the chill holiday-weekend vibes on Friday, as they held hands while out on a coffee date. For the outing, Zendaya layered a red-and-gold scarf over a loose gray T-shirt, straight-leg jeans, and black Converse sneakers, finishing the look with a black face mask. Holland wore a forest-green tee and dark jeans.

The Malcolm & Marie star also marked the special occasion on Instagram, sharing an adorable childhood pic where she's enjoying a day in the pool.
